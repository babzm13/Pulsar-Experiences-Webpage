Why Pulsar Experiences?

Why join our organization to run your LARP?

Choosing a parent organization, if any, to host your LARP project can be a difficult decision. We have some points about why Pulsar Experiences may or may not be right for you:

  • Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI): Pulsar Experiences is committed to creating and fostering LARPs with DEI elements. While not all LARPs can be for all people, we do believe most LARPs can do a damned good job of it. We strive to educate LARP runners and players about different DEI components, and how to talk to each other about needs and accomodations made to different people.
  • Child Friendliness: Not all games can be child-friendly and tell the stories they want to tell, but those that can, should, in our opinion. Children are the future of our games - we should attract as many of them to the hobby as we can as early as we can. They will grow up to become our story-tellers, our players and our creators soon enough.
  • A Compassionate and Competent Organization: Between Barb and Brian, we've done nearly every role you might expect to find in a LARP. If you have questions, we can answer them. If you need help finding people to fill roles, we may have suggestions. We're here to lend our expertise to your LARP, and help get it off the ground. We're also a friendly and fun pair who love to see other people's LARPs succeed. We're invested in you and your success!
  • The Staples: Insurance, Sites, Advertising, Etc: We provide the staples for games needing some extra help getting off the ground. We know a lot of sites in the area, and have good relationships with them. We also can provide insurance (with some caveats), and some advertising for your game as well. Who doesn't want that?

Does any of this ring true to you? If so, please reach out and contact us to talk about creating with us!
